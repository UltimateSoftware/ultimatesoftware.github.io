At Ultimate Software, we are a large company that truly puts its' People First. Our CEO is committed to putting People First - our turnover rate is less than 5% and we've never had any layoffs since our inception in 1990. We've got a unique company culture and many great perks such as a basketball court in the lobby of our HQ, and a massage therapist and wellness coach on staff. At Ultimate, your voice matters - we want to hear and implement your ideas, use cutting edge technologies, and continuously improve our cloud-based HR Management application. All the while, wanting you to have fun at work, enjoy our monthly free ice cream days, and spend valuable time away from work enjoying life in beautiful Miami area. For these reasons, as well as many more, Ultimate Software ranked # 20 in FORTUNE's Top 100 Best Companies to Work for in America for 2014 - we were ranked #9 in 2013!
